Helheim - FALLING INTO DARKNESS

The door to oblivion

Helheim is a realm where time has stopped and souls have lost their names. There is neither laughter nor crying here, only cold silence and shadows wandering aimlessly. Once upon a time, silence reigned here, and the laws of Hel guarded the balance between the world of the living and the dead. But everything has changed. The forces have split. Hel's servants are trying to maintain order, while the Souls of the Exiles seek to destroy all boundaries between life and death to put an end to eternal submission.
You stand in front of the gates of oblivion, a tall arch of black stone covered with runes that pulsate with ghostly light. You hear a whisper from behind the door. The voice of a shadow that knows your name offers two paths.

The right to peace of mind

You pass by endless alleys of souls standing in silent formation and head toward the throne of Hel, a majestic hall forged from petrified fog. Here sit the Judges, ancient beings who determine the fate of everyone who comes here. "Everyone must accept their fate. It is a law that cannot be broken," says one of them, as old as death itself.

But something is wrong. Darkness oozes from the walls. Shadows rise, whispering about freedom. The court will lose its power if the uprising is not stopped.

Dark rebellion

You descend into the lowlands, where faceless souls continue their endless march. The ice cracks underfoot, but they are unstoppable. They are led by a leader, someone who was once banished for disobedience. "Hel thinks her word is law. But who made her queen?" he says. His eyes burn with shadow, and his voice echoes in every soul.

This is not just a rebellion. It is a new vision of a world where no soul will be judged. Helheim is on the verge of splitting, and your choice will change everything.

The Battle of Helheim

The Palace of the Court is shrouded in darkness. On one side are the servants of order, on the other a crowd of outcasts. Sparks of spells and icy dust hang in the air. The city of lost souls trembles, ready to fall.

You are standing between two forces, and the whole world is frozen, waiting for your decision. Every step you take is a story that will affect millions of souls.

Texts of the forgotten

In the depths of the kingdom, there are libraries where no soul has set foot for thousands of years. Here you find scrolls carved into bones, books made of shadow skin, and voices speaking the language of the elders.
You find texts that reveal the truth: Helheim is not just a prison, but a place of transition. It cannot be destroyed, only understood.

A portal to oblivion

In the center of the kingdom is an ancient temple pulsating with dark light. It contains a Portal created by gods long forgotten. Through it, you can either preserve the boundaries or destroy the very essence of death.
The forces around you are raging - ancient spirits that do not know rest. The portal opens up your last choice.

The silent throne

Helheim is at peace. After battles, doubts, and choices, you stand before a throne from which Hel's voice no longer sounds. Everything is silent, but there is peace in this silence. Order has been restored, although the depths of the shadows still hold the echoes of war.

Now the choice is yours. Will you stay here to support the new order? Or will you go into the shadows, allowing the kingdom to live without a ruler?

Lord of the Dark

There is no law anymore. Order has been destroyed, but has chaos come? Souls do not wait for judgment-they choose their own path. Helheim has become a place of solutions, not punishments.
You are at the head of this new world, free and dangerous. And only you decide whether to be a ruler or to destroy even the idea of rules.
