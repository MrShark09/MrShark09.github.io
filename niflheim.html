Niflheim - THE SECRETS OF ETERNAL WINTER

The boundary of worlds

Niflheim is a realm of cold and forgotten souls, where time has no power. Once upon a time, peace reigned here, but now the gap between the world of the living and the dead is becoming dangerous. The Guardians of Darkness are trying to stop the chaos, and the Shadows of Oblivion are trying to completely destroy the boundary between the worlds. You stand before an icy monolith that radiates ancient magic. Its shadow extends into two different destinies.

The last defenders

You arrive at the Guardians' fortress, where black ice covers the ground. Warriors in dark armor are preparing for the most important battle of their lives.

"Our ancestors closed this portal. We cannot allow it to be opened," says the elder, holding a fragment of the seal. But cracks have already appeared in the walls of the fortress, and time is running out.

A breakthrough into the abyss

You make your way through the fog, where long-forgotten warriors are awakening. The mountain ranges are hidden in the darkness, and a voice whispers from the depths:

"The world of the living has locked us in darkness. It is time to break these chains." Shadows gather around you, ready to attack. You feel the border getting weaker.

The Battle of Niflheim

The world is splitting. Mountains are crumbling, ice is cracking, and darkness is swallowing up the last of the light. Shadows and Sentinels clash on the ice fields, where every step leaves bloody traces.

You are in the center of a decisive battle, and only your decision will determine what kind of world will emerge afterwards.

The Code of Darkness

Somewhere in the depths of the temple, hidden under an icy waterfall, scrolls containing the true nature of Niflheim are kept. Their pages are written in a language that only a select few understand. Only the one who reads them will understand the essence of this world - and will be able to change its fate. But are you ready to take on this burden?

The cursed seal

Deep in an icy crevasse, on the very edge of the worlds, is a symbol that stopped a catastrophe a millennium ago. But its magic is weakening. Destroying it means opening the way to the unknown. To strengthen it means to lose the chance for change forever.

The ice throne

The world of the living has won, but does this mean a true balance? Niflheim is frozen in a new era, where neither the living nor the dead are affected by chaos.

The fortresses are standing strong again, the magic is imprinted in the ice. Now it is your time to decide whether you will lead the realm of cold or disappear into its depths.

Lord of the Mists

The gap between the worlds no longer exists. The souls of the dead wander among the living, and the shadows are no longer hidden. Everything is mixed, and you are the one who leads the new order. But is it freedom or a new yoke? Niflheim is changed forever.

Final

“King of Winter” - You have become the Lord of Cold. A new order has been established between the worlds - harsh but fair. The border is closed, but the door is not forgotten. You rule from an icy throne, making sure that darkness does not creep in again. The cold preserves the memory of the war - and of you.

Final

“Disappearance without a Trace” - You leave everything behind. Your silhouette dissolves in the mists, and only the whisper of the wind recalls your name. History no longer remembers you, but it was you who stopped the chaos. No glory, no name, just a shadow in the icy darkness.

Final

“Master of Forgotten Souls” - You are leading a new era. Souls are no longer locked away, the living are not separated. Shadows dance next to the flame of life. But this world is incomprehensible and wild. You became its first ruler, the Lord of the forgotten souls, the creator of a new history.

Final

“The End of All Paths” - You have destroyed everything. The boundary is destroyed, the balance is lost. The world of Niflheim plunges into the abyss of chaos, where neither time nor death exists. Now there are neither living nor dead - there is only eternal winter, and you are its beginning.
