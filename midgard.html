MIDGARD - THE LOST FORGE OF THE GODS

Awakening in the temple

You open your eyes. Cold stones beneath you, the musty smell of antiquity in the air. Above your head are huge runes glowing with a faint blue light. Their glow makes your amulet pulsate. You don't remember who you are, but you're holding an old map in your hand. It leads to the lost forge of the gods.
Before you can realize anything, you hear footsteps outside the door. Odin's hunters. They are looking for you.

Meeting with a dwarf

You choose to escape. Making your way through the tunnel, you find yourself in an old dwarf forge. Here, the Guardian of the Forge is waiting for you, the last of the craftsmen who knows the truth about the lost place.

“Have you come for the Hammer of Storms?” he asks, looking at your amulet.
He tells you that the forge was sealed by the gods because it created weapons that could change reality itself.

The secret of the Valkyrie

You stand to the last. The hunters rush at you screaming. But before they can strike, the air is torn apart by the glitter of silver wings. A Valkyrie, Tordis, a warrior with eyes like a thunderstorm, descends from the heavens. She cuts the enemies with the lightning of her sword.

“If you are here, then you have a map,” she says. “It leads to the lost weapon of the gods. Let me come with you.”

Secrets of the forge

You set off with the Guardian. You cross caves, bridges over lava rivers, and finally enter the lost forge itself. Inside, amidst the quiet flames, stands the Hammer of Storms, an artifact forged in the very depths of time.

“This hammer is made for great things,” says Dwarf. “But its power can destroy the world or save it.”

Hunting for an artifact

Together with Tordis, you head to the forge. She is silent most of the way, but later confesses:
“I'm not just a Valkyrie. I remember that war. I saw what this weapon is capable of. It should belong neither to gods nor to mortals. Only I can contain its power.”
Her words sound honest. Or maybe they sound cunning.

Alone against the gods

You choose to go alone. You cross the endless ruins and reach the temple of records. There, among the dust and stone tablets, you learn the terrible truth: you forged the Hammer of Storms yourself. You were a master of the gods. But Odin erased your memory because you became too powerful.
Now the choice is yours.

The battle for the artifact

You've decided to find your own strength. The Valkyrie Tordis stands in your way, her eyes burning with determination. “I will not allow you to repeat the mistakes of the gods,” she says. But you can't trust anyone. The artifact is too powerful to belong to anyone.

A fierce battle begins between you. The Hammer of Storms pulsates with energy, witchcraft dust rises into the air, and weapon strikes break the silence of the ancient forge. And in this battle, not only the fate of the weapon is decided - the fate of the world is decided.

Final

“Heir to the Forge” - You accept the power of the Hammer and swear to use it wisely. The forge comes to life, the flame burns again. You are the new Guardian. You are the new Guardian.

Final

“Free from burden” - You break the Hammer and destroy the forge. The world will not get this power - and perhaps this will save it.

Final

“Curse of the Gods” - Tordis receives an artifact. The world is changing. The balance is collapsing. And no one knows what will happen next.

Final

“The Blacksmith's Revival” - You regain your name, your strength, your skill. The forge breathes again, and you become the man you were meant to be.

Final

“Challenge to the Gods” - You go to Asgard with anger and truth in your heart. A new fire of war is burning in the heavens.
